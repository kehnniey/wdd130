Most popular rafting trips

American River Rafting: Raft the South Fork or the Middle Fork of the American River for a classic California whitewater experience.

Yosemite Whitewater Rafting: It’s an exhilarating ride on the Merced, Tuolumne or Cherry Creek while visiting Yosemite National Park.

Multi-Day Rafting Trips: Immerse yourself in the ultimate outdoor experience California rivers have to offer - overnight rafting trips.
